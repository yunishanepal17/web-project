Birat Multiple College

Birat Multiple College was established in 2012 AD (2069 BS) as a private base Science a nd Technology college in Biratnagar. It is located in Mangadh Marg, Chandani Chowk, Bargachi, Biratnagar, Morang district of Nepal. It is affiliated to Tribhuvan University and recognized from University Grant Commission of Nepal. It was founded with the mission to develop a center of academic excellence on value integrating general education, and professional skills. It offers Bachelor degree programs in B.Sc. Computer Science and Informatio n Technology (B.SC. CSIT), B.Tech. Food Technology, and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Microbiology.
